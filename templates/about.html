Overuse of Medicine: The Silent Pandemic

Published on Economic Times Health

Introduction

The overuse of medicine, especially among older adults, is becoming a silent pandemic. "Polypharmacy" involves taking multiple medications, which can lead to severe consequences like falls, fractures, hospitalizations, and even death.

Deprescribing: A Solution

"Deprescribing" involves reviewing medications and stopping unnecessary ones. It has shown to reduce harmful drug interactions and improve overall health outcomes.

Predicting Accidental Drug Overdose Using SPOT

Published on PMC - National Center for Biotechnology Information

How SPOT Works

SPOT (Suspected Potential Overdose Tracker) predicts accidental drug overdoses in near real-time using public health data. It enables quicker identification of overdose fatalities.

Public Health Implications

Rapid overdose death predictions allow authorities to implement timely interventions, monitor trends, and assess prevention programs' effectiveness.
