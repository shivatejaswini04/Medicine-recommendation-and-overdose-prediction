🦠 Predicted Disease: GERD (Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease)

What is GERD?

💊 GERD is a chronic condition where stomach acid frequently flows back into the esophagus, irritating the lining. Symptoms include heartburn, regurgitation, chest pain, and difficulty swallowing.

Management & Lifestyle Tips

🍽️ Treatment includes dietary changes like avoiding spicy food, caffeine, and lying down after meals. Medications such as antacids and proton pump inhibitors are also commonly used. In severe cases, surgery may be considered.
